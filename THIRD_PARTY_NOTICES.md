## Council

[`tsenart/council-skill`](https://github.com/tsenart/council-skill), licensed
under the MIT License.

The adapted version preserves the original council concept and protocol while
adding stricter real-delegation requirements, semantic multilingual triad

The upstream license is included at `skills/council/LICENSE`.

## Self-learning and Authoring Skills

`self-learning` is adapted from
[`kulaxyz/self-learning-skills`](https://github.com/kulaxyz/self-learning-skills),
licensed under the MIT License. The local adaptation separates the authoring
rubric, template, and per-agent guidance into the required sibling
`authoring-skills` skill. `authoring-skills` is derived from that upstream
authoring material and expanded with cross-agent review guidance.

The upstream license is included in both skill directories.
